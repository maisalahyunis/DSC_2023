Decision Support Center

The Decision Support Center (DSC) was established by the Council of Ministers Resolution No. (337). It is regulatorily linked to the Royal Court and financially and administratively independent. It aims at supporting the decision-making process and improving its effectiveness and efficiency across different areas in a timely and precise manner through scientific and practical means and mechanisms that ensure making and executing the appropriate decisions.

Business Areas

Conducting Developmental Studies

Conducting studies with regards to high priority development topics and the obstacles, difficulties, and problems in their way and providing the necessary recommendations to face them.

Conducting Proactive Studies

Following and analyzing the latest developments and potential challenges across different fields and providing the necessary recommendations to face them.

Monitoring and Analyzing

Monitoring and analyzing events; developments; changes; phenomena; as well as internal, regional, and external issues and their implications, and offering the suitable options and alternatives in their regard.

Providing Information to Support Government Entities’ Outcomes

Contributing to make the necessary information available to the Council of Ministers, Political and Security Affairs Council, and Economical and Development Affairs Council to support them in fulfilling their mandates.

Gathering and Analyzing Data

Gathering, documenting, analyzing, processing and storing data and information related to the Center’s functions and its assigned tasks and requests.

Participating in Educating the Public Opinion

Participating in educating the public opinion and raising its awareness regarding the made decisions and raised topics and issues.

Graduates Development Program

Inspired by the Kingdom’s Vision 2030 and its strong belief in the capabilities of Saudi youth, the Center seeks to enable fresh graduates and develop their skills and knowledge in its areas of specialization.
