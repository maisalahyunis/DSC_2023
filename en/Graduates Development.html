Introduction to the Graduate Development Program (GDP) page: As part of the Kingdom's Vision 2030 and its continuous effort to harness the potential of young national competencies, the Decision Support Center (DSC) seeks to empower fresh graduates and develop their skills and knowledge within its specializations. DSC executes an annual GDP program based on specialized tracks in collaboration with distinguished international universities, to equip fresh graduates with the essential skills sought after in the job market as a steppingstone for their professional career path.
